About Us

History of WegoWin

WegoWin was formed by 5 people who have different educational backgrounds. And was created on June 24, 2020 in Indonesia, Sidoarjo. The creation of WegoWin was not without reason, as the founders of WegoWin saw how low incomes in a country and the high unemployment. WegoWin hopes it's all gone

Goal of WegoWin

WegoWin's goal to be created is because it wants to advance a country and its people both individually and in groups in the economic sector. WegoWin is very ambitious with this and hopes the whole community can support it. And in the end hope will always be beneficial for the common good.

Teams of WegoWin

WegoWin has a large number of teams consisting of several members. In WegoWin there are 6 teams namely IT team, product marketing team, product business team, editing team, development team, learning team, and admin team. They will always collaborate for WegoWin's progress

Partners of WegoWin

WegoWin has many partners who are very trusted and very helpful in the development of learning platforms in WegoWin. They are very ambitious in investing their knowledge in WegoWin because all the mentors at WegoWin are both businesses and WegoWin partners.

Study Leadership of WegoWin

Leadership learning exercises at WegoWin are highly prioritized and closely observed by all WegoWin residents. Because leadership is a supply and a start to start something. based on the leadership of all things will become realized

Features of WegoWin

WegoWin has many features to access a material even practice to directly apply the science that has been given. There will even be some new science to be given to mentors. And it doesn't close the possibility that successful members can also share their success tips
